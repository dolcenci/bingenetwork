binge tech entreneurship growth network.

The binge tech entrepreneurship growth network. A well knitted collection of events that are created with the aim of making sure that small to medium bussinesses thrive regardless of the environment they are in.

Founded in 2019, it has helped both the young and the old, the enlightened and the ingenious and dont forget the doubtful along with the enthusiastic.

Ranging from the eyecatching keynote that was delivered this year to the much helpful final event  that will take place near the end of the year, this network  is the journey we will walk together and will hopefully cherish.
